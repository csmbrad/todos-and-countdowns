The To Do List:

The To-Do List allows you to add items with a short name (less than 100 characters) and a length of time to complete them (shorter than 12 hours). The list displays how long all your tasks will take based on your estimates and also displays the time you will be done based on your estimates. This is shown in your local time. These times ignore completed entries. All your entries will remain intact between logins and devices as long as you use the same account.

Adding items to the To-Do List:

Adding items to the list is as simple as filling out the form and pressing submit. The title field takes text and is limited to 100 characters. The duration field takes a number, in hours, between 0.5 and 12. It only accepts numbers in increments of half an hour, like 1.5, 2.5, and so forth. Once you have entered your information press the big green submit button.

Editing items from the To-Do List:

You can edit an item by the to-do list by clicking on the Edit button in its entry. This will highlight it in blue, and change the main form to an edit form with the same blue background. It will automatically populate the form with the values from the entry. You can edit these values, then press the big green submit button to edit the entry, or you can switch to another entry to edit by pressing its edit button. You cannot edit, complete, or delete an entry while editing it.

Deleting items from the To-Do List:

You can delete an item by pressing the Delete button on its entry and confirming by pressing "Ok" in the popup. BE WARNED, this process will delete your entry FOREVER and cannot be reversed.

Completing an item on the To-Do List:

You can mark an item as complete by pressing the green Complete! button in its entry. The item will be colored light green and the button will change to an Uncomplete button. Completed entries will stay completed even after refreshing the page, and will not be counted towards the time to finish.

The Countdowns:

The countdown section allows you to set daily "countdowns" or "alarms" that will display how much time is left until a specific time of day. They reset automatically once their target time is reached. The time remaining until the target time is displayed visually below its numeric representation. These countdowns are useful because they can help you easily see how much time you have before a deadline, class, video game daily reset, or any other event that occurs at a specific time.

Adding a Countdown:

Adding a countdown is very similar to adding a to-do item. The countdown form needs to be filled out with a name (between 1 and 100 characters), the target hour from 0 to 23, and the minute within that target hour from 0 to 59. These numbers must be whole numbers, like 1, 15, 23, etc. Once you have provided this information, click the big green submit button and the countdown will be added.
